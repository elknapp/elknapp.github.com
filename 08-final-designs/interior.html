Kling Magazine

A project done for typography III, students chose a brand for a fashion catalogue and paired it with a concept and written piec of their choosing. I chose Kling, a brand based in Spain because I visited the store when I was in Dublin. The concept behind the catalogue is based on your grandmother's closet. Basically it follows this idea of dress-up and the transition into adulthood. The catalogue leads the viewer through this transition with photographs in frame and pictures in an album. The catalogue features vintage inspired clothing set with excerpts from the Seventeen guide to Beauty and Fashion, a book written in the 60's that gives fashion and etiquette advice.

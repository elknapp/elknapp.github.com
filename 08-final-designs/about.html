Three to One

"For every act of kindness you receive, give three acts in return."

Who We Are

Three to One is a site dedicated to the creation of patterns inspired by keepsakes.

How it Works

Upload a photo of your keepsake to the site and use the pattern generator to make a pattern. Finally, share! We encourage our users to share their patterns with others. Apply it to one of our products and give it as gift or allow other users to download the image.

Hello!

who we are

3:1 is a website dedicated to the creation of patterns inspired by keepsakes.

how it works

Hit upload and you will be re-directed to our pattern generator. Simply upload a photo of your keepsake and the pattern generator will create a pattern based on a series of filters controlled by you. Finally, share! We encourage users to share their patterns with others. Apply the pattern to one of our products or allow other users to download it. This site runs purely on donations, users can pay whatever the want for the pattern, even $0 for a free download. All funds from the sales go to running the site.
